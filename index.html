Our Mission

Women Owned. Environmentally Focused. Technology Driven.

At GreenGrid we strive to create a world in which clean and renewable energy is available to everyone. Our focus is on the environment and ensuring we protect the natural beauty and resources around us.

Our solar panels are made of lightweight, durable materials built to last. With weather resistant technology you will never need to worry. All components are ethically sourced, made from recycled materials, and contain no toxic chemicals. Perfect to put on your home, backyard, and more. With their versatility they can bring you power anywhere you can imagine.
